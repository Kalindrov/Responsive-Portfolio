About Me

Former Lead Game Designer turned Full Stack Developer. I wanted to explore the other side of development, so here I am. You have reached my portfolio of work that showcases my skill set. I lean towards becoming a front-end developer due to my experience in UI/UX design in games. I have over 12 years experience designing games for platforms such as PC, console, web, facebook, and mobile. I am taking my knowledge of how to lead design teams, understand product management and resources into my new venture in web development.
